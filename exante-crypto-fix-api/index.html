Exante Crypto and FIX API

Exante Crypto, a cryptocurrency exchange and leading fintech company, today announced its arm of institutional services for digital assets. Exante Crypto‘s launch of FIX API, further accelerates the adoption from its growing list of institutional customers, who want to add Bitcoin and other cryptocurrency trading strategies to their offerings.

Exante Crypto‘s FIX API trading, is available to institutional investors, brokers, private traders, hedge funds, asset managers, corporations, VCs, high net worth individuals, endowments, family offices and private wealth managers.

With FIX engine integration, institutions can take advantage of minimal latency, secure connection and full-time access around the clock. Institutions will have access to both market rates and trading with full order books, from Exante Crypto‘s over 75 markets. Institutions will also have access to the most popular order execution types.

“We’re seeing institutional adoption of crypto and digital assets on a global scale,” said the head marketer of Exante Crypto. “Exante Crypto‘s mission is to make both retail and institutional access to crypto easy, secure and reliable. Exante Crypto‘s release of the FIX API provides institutions with the ability to manage all their crypto positions programmatically. The launch of our FIX API, demonstrates our commitment to providing customers with institutional standards of speed, connectivity, security and compliance that institutional traders rely on.”

He continued, “We anticipate our first global institution to begin trading on our FIX API by the end of April, with many more to be added in the coming months.”
